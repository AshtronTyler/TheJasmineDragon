OUR MISSION

Our mission here at The Jasmine Dragon is to help reunite the four nations after this time of war and destruction. The Fire Nation has all but decimated The Four Nations and we would like to offer a place of peace and community. Let this be a place for people to gather and learn from one another as opposed to being divided from each other. We wish for this to be a place of knowledge, inclusivity, and happiness. In an effort to spread joy across the land, we are now offfering to deliver our tea leaves for those of you who cannot make the travel to Ba Sing Se. We hope this mission is enough to remedy the harm we have caused to you and yours and we will never stop fighting to bring peace and balance to The Four Nations.

Regions

In an effort to represent The Four Nations, each of our teas reside from different regions of the land. Just as we all have unique characteristics and traits, our teas also have differing flavor profiles, colors, and strengths depending on what region the leaves are grown in.

OUR TEAS


JASMINE PEARL

Region: Republic City

Our Jasmine Pearl blend is our pick of the year! It's fragrant is sweet and sugary while it is light and airy on the palate.

BUTTERFLY PEA

Region: Southern Air Temple

Butterfly Pea Flower is the perfect addition to anything acidic. It goes great with lemonade and will add a beautiful blue hue to any beverage.

Chai

Region: Northern Fire Nation

Chai, just like the fire nation, can be a bit spicy! It is the perfect drink to sip if you want to feel warm on the inside and out.

SNAIL KEEMUN

Region: Omashu

Snail Keemun has notes that are brisk, bold, and toasty. This is a great for entertaining guests in the early fall!

ROYAL STAR

Region: Eastern Air Temple

This tea is closely related to Oolong as it is built of the base of steamed Oolong leaves. It is then blended with sweet peach and spices to give in a refined finsih. Perfect for impressing that special someone!

WHITE ROSE

Region: Northern Water Tribe.

White Rose is perfect for that mid-day tea. It is light and refreshing and will be sure to take the stress away from whatever you are dealing with!
